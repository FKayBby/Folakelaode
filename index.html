Meet Folake Laode

Folake Laode is a versatile compere, event host, broadcaster, and voice-over artist whose career reflects depth, diversity, and a lifelong devotion to growth. With a commanding presence, she has hosted dazzling red-carpet events, engaged audiences as a radio personality, and brought structure, warmth, and elegance to weddings, birthdays, and corporate occasions. Her hallmark is authenticity; turning every event into an unforgettable experience.

A graduate of Computer Science, Folake’s journey has always been anchored in education. She has taught undergraduate students in polytechnic, guiding them through practicals in computer technology, and continues to see teaching as a calling as much as a profession. For her, going to school and pursuing knowledge is not just a path to advancement but a source of joy and fulfillment.

Professionally, Folake has invested in extensive training, sharpening her craft through dedicated programs in broadcasting and voice-over artistry. Today, she is not only a skilled voice-over artist but also a mentor who has trained others in mastering communication and presentation.

A devoted reader with an insatiable appetite for books, Folake continues to expand her horizons through the written word. Reading is more than a pastime for her; it enriches her voice, her hosting, and her worldview.

Her leadership extends to the creative industry, where she serves as the Chief Whip of the Association of Voice-over Artists (AVOA), Nigeria, under the leadership of renowned veteran actor, Mr. Segun Arinze.

For her, every microphone is a classroom, every event a stage for connection, and every book a new horizon.
